ABOUT US

We are one of the Clubs among the clubs present on the African continent and the Arab world affiliated with the ACPC, which is the African Arab collegiate Programming Competition.Our role is to impart full knowledge of the competition and its various stages, and to conduct training to teach programming and its most important skills, which are Problem Solving, and to facilitate participation in the competition for students of the Academy

What is Problem Solving ?

Solving problems is the core of computer science. Programmers must first understand how a human solves a problem, then understand how to translate this "algorithm" into something a computer can do, and finally how to "write" the specific syntax (required by a computer) to get the job done.

What is Competitive Programming ?

Programming is fun, programming is an exercise for your brain, programming is a mental sport and when this sport is held over the internet involving sport programmer as a contestant then it is called Competitive Programming

why competitive programming ?

it Makes you a desirable candidate to major companies,Makes you faster and more focused and Teaches you how to work in teams
